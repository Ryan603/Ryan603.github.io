About Me

More Than Headlines

I'm Ryan Wangari, a multimedia journalist and storyteller based in Nairobi, Kenya. My work sits at the intersection of journalism, documentary film, and audio production — spaces where I believe the most honest human stories live.

My career has taken me from the lakeside communities of Kapiyo to the studios of Ruben FM, from festival stages at Vuka 2025 to post-production suites handling internationally-distributed podcast content. Along the way, I've earned recognition as a Best Sound Design and Best Film Editing Runner-Up honoree, while working alongside teams that have claimed Best Documentary, Best Director, and Best Production Design at the same festival.

Whether I'm behind a camera, inside a recording booth, or shaping the narrative arc of a 30-minute documentary — my commitment is the same: tell the truth, beautifully.

"Every story has someone who hasn't been heard yet. My job is to find them, earn their trust, and make the world listen."
